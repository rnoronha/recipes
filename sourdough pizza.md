# Sourdough Pizza:
## Ingredients: 

* 250g starter at 100% hydration by weight
* 120g water
* 300g flour
* 1 tsp salt

## Recipe

1. Mix everything
2. Knead until smooth
3. Rise 2-4 hours
4. Punch down, leave in fridge overnight
5. Bake (with toppings) for 5-15 minutes at 450f

Yields either two 12" pizzas or one 14" pizza
